How we met

Jessica and Parker grew up in Murfreesboro, TN and have known each other since at least 2nd grade. Separate paths lead us both to Denver, CO where we reunited over our passion for winter sports. Parker moved out to Denver, Colorado after graduating college in 2014 to enjoy all things the mountain life offered. I lived in Nashville for a few years until I started travel nursing where I lived all over the United States before taking an assignment in Denver. We reunited over a mutual passion for winter sports, though Parker, being a snowboarder, jokes that he never thought his favorite person to go to the mountains with would be a skier.
